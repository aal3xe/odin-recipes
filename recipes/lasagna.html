Lasagna

Description

This white chicken lasagna recipe is a satisfying and crowd-pleasing twist on a classic Italian favorite.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Buter
  • Vegetables
  • Garlic
  • Chicken
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Boil and drain the lasagna noodles.
  2. Cook the onion and garlic in butter
  3. Add the broth, milk, and salt.
  4. Add some of the mozzarella and Parmesan
  5. Season with basil, oregano, and pepper.
  6. Assemble the lasagna according to the recipe
  7. Bake in the preheated oven
